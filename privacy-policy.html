2.Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent. PDFCraft Pro is designed as a fully offline, on-device application. We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure that stores your personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any registration details
  • Your documents, PDF files, scanned images, or any file contents you create or process
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your contacts, calendar, or messages
  • Your microphone input or audio recordings
  • Biometric data such as fingerprints or facial recognition templates
  • Your browsing history or activity outside the App
  • Unique device identifiers for personal profiling by us

All document processing, PDF creation, compression, merging, editing, and OCR operations are performed entirely on your device. Your files never leave your device through our App.

3.Information That May Be Collected Automatically

While we do not collect personal data ourselves, certain third-party services integrated into PDFCraft Pro may automatically collect limited, non-personally-identifying technical data. This includes:

  • Crash Reports: Anonymised crash logs may be transmitted to help us identify and fix technical issues. These logs contain device type, Android version, and a stack trace — no personal content.
  • Ad-related Data: Google AdMob, our advertising partner, may collect certain device and usage information for the purpose of displaying relevant advertisements. See Section 7 for full details.
  • App Performance Data: Aggregated, anonymised usage statistics may be collected to improve the App experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
4.Camera Permission

PDFCraft Pro requests access to your device camera solely for the purpose of document scanning. The camera is activated only when you explicitly open the Smart Scanner or ID Card Scanner feature by tapping the respective button.

Images captured through the camera are processed entirely on your device using on-device algorithms for edge detection, perspective correction, and image enhancement. Camera images are never transmitted to any external server by our App.

We do not record video, take photos in the background, or access the camera at any time other than when you explicitly initiate a scan. You may revoke the camera permission at any time through your device's Settings without affecting other App features.

5.Storage Permission

PDFCraft Pro requests access to your device storage in order to read files you select for processing (such as selecting a PDF to compress or merge) and to save the output files (PDFs, images) to your device. This is the core functionality of the App.

We only access files that you explicitly select through the system file picker. We do not scan, index, read, upload, or transmit any files stored on your device beyond what you directly choose to process within the App.

Output files are saved to either your public Documents/PDFCraft folder or the App's private storage, depending on your chosen setting. You can manage and delete all output files at any time through the App's File Manager.

6.Biometric Permission (Secure Vault)

The Secure Vault feature uses your device's biometric authentication system (fingerprint or face recognition) as an additional layer of security. PDFCraft Pro does not capture, store, or process your biometric data in any way.

All biometric authentication is handled entirely by the Android BiometricPrompt API, which operates within your device's secure hardware enclave (Trusted Execution Environment). Your biometric data never leaves this secure enclave and is never accessible to our App.

The vault password you create is stored in the App's encrypted SharedPreferences using Android's SecurityCrypto library with AES-256-GCM encryption. This password is never transmitted externally.

7.Advertising — Google AdMob

PDFCraft Pro is a free application supported by advertising provided by Google AdMob, a service of Google LLC. AdMob may collect and use certain data about you and your device to display personalised advertisements.

The data AdMob may collect includes:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable, anonymous identifier assigned by your device)
  • Device type, operating system, and language settings
  • IP address (used to estimate general location for ad targeting)
  • App interactions and usage patterns for ad frequency capping

This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via your device's Settings > Privacy > Ads and resetting or opting out of your Advertising ID.

8.Google ML Kit — On-Device OCR

PDFCraft Pro uses Google ML Kit for Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and document scanning. ML Kit processes data entirely on-device. No images or document content are sent to Google's servers for ML Kit processing.

ML Kit may download model files to your device when the App is first used for OCR or scanning. These are small AI model files and contain none of your personal data. Once downloaded, all processing occurs locally without further network communication.

9.Cloud Backup Feature

PDFCraft Pro includes an optional Cloud Backup feature powered by Google Drive. This feature is entirely opt-in and requires you to sign in with your Google account.

If you choose to use this feature, your PDF files will be uploaded to your personal Google Drive account. We do not have access to your Google Drive or the files stored there. You can disconnect your Google account from the App at any time through the backup settings.

10.Internet Permission

PDFCraft Pro requires internet permission for the following specific purposes only:

  • Loading and displaying Google AdMob advertisements
  • Downloading ML Kit AI model files on first use
  • Optional Google Drive backup (only when you explicitly initiate a backup)
  • Checking network connectivity status

The internet permission is not used to transmit your documents, files, or personal data to any server operated by S&AA Play.

11.Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect information within the App:

  • Password-locked PDFs are encrypted using AES-128 encryption
  • The Secure Vault master password is stored using AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Biometric authentication uses your device's hardware-backed secure enclave
  • No sensitive data is transmitted over unencrypted connections

Important: We strongly advise you to maintain secure backups of important documents. We cannot be held responsible for data loss due to device failure, factory resets, or accidental deletion.
